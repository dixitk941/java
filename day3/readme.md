### Comparison Operators

* `==`: equal to
* `!=`: not equal to
* `>`: greater than
* `<`: less than
* `>=` : greater than or equal to
* `<=`: less than or equal to

### Logical Operators

* `&&`: logical and
* `||`: logical or
* `!`: logical not

### Assignment Operators

* `=`: assignment
* `+=` : addition assignment
* `-=`: subtraction assignment
* `*=`: multiplication assignment
* `/=`: division assignment
* `%=` : modulus assignment
* `<<=`: left shift assignment
* `>>=`: right shift assignment
